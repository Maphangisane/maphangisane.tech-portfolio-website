About Me

Get to know about me

I'm Professional Software Developer with formal education, bootcamps, and workplace Experience.

Hi, I’m John Doe, a passionate software developer with over 7 years of experience in building innovative and efficient software solutions. I specialize in full-stack development, with a keen interest in creating responsive web applications and robust backend systems. I started my career in software development 7 years ago, working with various companies ranging from startups to large enterprises. Over the years, I have honed my skills in web development, backend systems, and cloud computing, and have successfully delivered projects that meet client requirements and exceed expectations. My technical toolkit includes a diverse range of programming languages and frameworks such as JavaScript, Python, React, Java, and Spring-boot. I am particularly proficient in developing scalable web applications, microservices architecture, and RESTful API design. I hold a Bachelor’s Degree in Computer Science from University of Limpopo. Additionally, I am certified as an AWS Certified Solutions Architect and a Google Professional Cloud Developer. These educational experiences have provided me with a solid foundation in both theoretical and practical aspects of software development. When I’m not coding, you can find me exploring the latest tech trends, participating in the EPL Fantasy League, hackathons, contributing to open-source projects, or enjoying outdoor activities like soccer and Playing Games. These interests help me stay creative and motivated in my professional life. Feel free to reach out to me at johndoe@example.com or connect with me on LinkedIn. I’m always open to discussing new projects, creative ideas, or opportunities to be part of your vision.
