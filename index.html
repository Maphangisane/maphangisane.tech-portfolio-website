Who Am I

About Me

Get to know about me

Hi, I’m Thabang Hezekiel Maphangisane, a passionate software developer with a solid foundation in computer science, thanks to my BSc in Mathematical Sciences from the University of Limpopo. I specialize in full-stack development, with a strong focus on building responsive web applications and scalable backend systems.

Currently, I am furthering my expertise through the ALX Holberton Full Stack Software Engineering Programme, where I am specializing in Backend development. My technical skills span a diverse range of programming languages and frameworks, including Java, Spring Boot, JavaScript, Python, and React.

During my internship at WITS University Tshimologong Digital Skills Academy, I gained hands-on experience in both front-end and back-end development. I contributed to various projects, which not only honed my ability to deliver efficient software solutions but also enhanced my teamwork, communication, problem-solving, and adaptability skills.

I’m always eager to connect with like-minded professionals and explore new opportunities. Feel free to reach out to me at maphangisane@gmail.com or connect with me on LinkedIn.
